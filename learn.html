The Course

What You'll Walk Away With

A full day of learning, laughing, and creating — with everything you need to start making professional-quality cookies at home.

01

The Perfect Shortbread Base

Gwensi's tried-and-tested shortbread recipe — the crisp, buttery foundation that holds up to detailed decoration without crumbling.

02

Mixing Royal Icing

Learn to mix royal icing to the exact right consistency for flooding, outlining, and detail work. Get the colours right every time.

03

Outlining & Flooding

Master the two-stage technique — create clean outlines and flood fills that dry smooth and professional. No lumps, no gaps.

04

Detail Work & Texture

Add personalisation — names, patterns, characters, and texture effects like the crinkle-paper technique Gwensi is known for.

05

Packaging & Gifting

Learn how to store, package, and present your cookies beautifully — so they look just as good in a gift box as in a photo.

06

Take Your Creations Home

Everything you make on the day is yours to take home. You'll leave with a full box of your own hand-decorated cookies.
