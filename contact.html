Frequently Asked Questions

All orders require a minimum of 5 days advance notice before your collection or delivery date. This gives us the time to source the best ingredients, design your cake properly, and bake and decorate it with the care it deserves. For large or complex orders (multi-tier, sculpted cakes, or large sweet tables) we recommend ordering 7–14 days in advance.

We offer 16 flavours, all included in the cake price at no extra charge: Vanilla, Chocolate, Coffee, Carrot, Choc Chip, Choc & Caramel, Peanut Butter, Cookies & Cream, Vanilla with Oreo, Strawberry, Lemon, Orange, Red Velvet, Blue Velvet, Salted Caramel, and Marble. Our most popular are Salted Caramel, Red Velvet, and Choc & Caramel.

Our cakes are priced by size — from R250 for a 12cm cake up to R900+ for large or tiered designs. One tier equals three layers, and your chosen flavour is always included in the price. Highly detailed, sculpted, or multi-tier cakes are quoted individually based on complexity and design requirements. See our full pricing page or WhatsApp us for a personalised quote.
