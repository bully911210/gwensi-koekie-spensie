Good to Know

A few important notes before you order

5-Day Advance Notice: All orders must be placed at least 5 days before your collection or delivery date. This ensures we can give your order the attention and care it deserves.

Payment Upfront: Full payment is required to confirm your order. We accept EFT and cash on collection. Payment confirms your date and slot.

Custom Designs: Highly detailed, sculpted or tiered designs are individually quoted based on complexity. WhatsApp us with your idea for a personalised quote.

Collection & Delivery: We are primarily a collection service — please arrange a time to collect. Delivery via Uber is available as an option, but this is arranged and managed entirely at the client's own risk. We are not liable for any damage during transit.
